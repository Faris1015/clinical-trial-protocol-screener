# Protocol XYZ-2024-001: A Phase II Study of Osimertinib in EGFR-Mutated NSCLC

## Section 5. Study Population

### 5.1 Inclusion Criteria

Patients must meet all of the following to be eligible:

1. Age >= 18 years at the time of consent.
2. Histologically confirmed non-small cell lung cancer, stage IV.
3. Documented EGFR exon 19 deletion or L858R mutation.
4. ECOG performance status <= 1.
5. Adequate renal function.
6. Left ventricular ejection fraction >= 50%.

### 5.2 Exclusion Criteria

Patients are excluded if any of the following apply:

1. Prior EGFR TKI therapy.
2. Systolic blood pressure > 160 mmHg despite antihypertensive treatment.
3. Absolute neutrophil count < 1.5 x 10^9/L.
4. Women of childbearing potential who are pregnant or breastfeeding.

*Note: this sample is intentionally written so "adequate renal function" (Inclusion #5)
is vague — the Critic rule RENAL-001 will reject the first parse and force a corrected
extraction with a numeric eGFR threshold.*
