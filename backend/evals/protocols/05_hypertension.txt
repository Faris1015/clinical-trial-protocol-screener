A Phase III study of a fixed-dose combination antihypertensive in adults with
stage 2 hypertension.

Inclusion Criteria:
1. Age 18 years or older.
2. Systolic blood pressure of 140 mmHg or higher.
3. Diastolic blood pressure of 90 mmHg or higher.
4. Body mass index of 40 kg/m2 or less.

Exclusion Criteria:
1. Secondary hypertension.
2. Currently pregnant or breastfeeding.
