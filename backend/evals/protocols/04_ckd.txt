A Phase III study of a renoprotective agent in adults with chronic kidney
disease.

Inclusion Criteria:
1. Age 18 years or older.
2. Estimated glomerular filtration rate between 25 and 75 mL/min/1.73m2.
3. Serum creatinine of 3.0 mg/dL or less.

Exclusion Criteria:
1. Diagnosis of polycystic kidney disease.
2. Received a kidney transplant.
