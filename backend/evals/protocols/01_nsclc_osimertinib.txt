A Phase III, randomized study of osimertinib in patients with EGFR-mutated
non-small cell lung cancer.

Inclusion Criteria:
1. Age 18 years or older at the time of informed consent.
2. Histologically confirmed non-small cell lung cancer.
3. Documented EGFR exon 19 deletion or L858R substitution mutation.
4. ECOG performance status of 0 or 1.
5. Absolute neutrophil count greater than or equal to 1.5 x10^9/L.
6. Platelet count greater than or equal to 100 x10^9/L.

Exclusion Criteria:
1. Prior treatment with any EGFR tyrosine kinase inhibitor.
2. Symptomatic central nervous system metastases.
