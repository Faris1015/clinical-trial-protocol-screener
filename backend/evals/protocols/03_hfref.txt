A Phase II study of a novel agent in patients with heart failure with reduced
ejection fraction.

Inclusion Criteria:
1. Age 21 years or older.
2. Chronic heart failure diagnosis for at least 6 months.
3. Left ventricular ejection fraction of 40% or less.
4. Systolic blood pressure of at least 100 mmHg.

Exclusion Criteria:
1. Systolic blood pressure greater than 180 mmHg.
2. Adequate hepatic function is required, per investigator judgment.
