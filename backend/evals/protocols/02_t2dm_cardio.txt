A Phase IV cardiovascular outcomes trial of an SGLT2 inhibitor in adults with
type 2 diabetes mellitus.

Inclusion Criteria:
1. Age between 40 and 80 years, inclusive.
2. Diagnosis of type 2 diabetes mellitus.
3. Glycated hemoglobin (HbA1c) of 7.0% or higher.
4. Body mass index of 25 kg/m2 or greater.

Exclusion Criteria:
1. Estimated glomerular filtration rate below 30 mL/min/1.73m2.
2. Current treatment with an SGLT2 inhibitor.
